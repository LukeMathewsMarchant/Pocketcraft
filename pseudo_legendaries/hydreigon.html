Deino Spawn Locations

  • Deino is a rare spawn in the deep dark biome and this is the best place to find one

    • Deino spawns in a very dangerous biome, however if you can make the deep dark biome safe by building a shelter or removing the dangerous blocks, it won't be too difficult to obtain one.

    The best way to catch a Deino is to hunt in a deep dark biome. After you make the biome safe the biggest challenge will just be not getting bored and quitting the hunt. To keep yourself entertained while you wait for a deino to spawn, I recommend building a cool structure, mining for resources or training your Pokemon. Also you could just watch Netflix in the background haha.

    I highly recommend using the pokenav mod so you don't waste time hunting in an area where Deino can't actually spawn.

    My favorite structure to build in biomes while I'm hunting for rare Pokemon like Deino is a Pokemon Center. It helps the world come alive and feel more like a true Pokemon game. The video below gives a step-by-step tutorial on how to build a Pokemon Center if you would like to do so while hunting Deino.

    Evolving Deino to Hydreigon

    Deino evolves into Zweilous at level 50, and then into Hydreigon at level 64.

    You can use experience shares to help raise your deino's level more easily. Plains and snowy biomes are the best places to train if you want to level up your Pokemon quickly. I recommend targeting high-level common pokemon like Tauros and Wyrdeer.
