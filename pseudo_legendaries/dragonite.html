Why Dragonite is a Must-Have Pseudo-Legendary

Dragonite is one of the strongest pseudo‑legendary Pokémon available in Cobblemon. Its high stats and powerful moves make it a top-tier addition to any Cobblemon team.

Dratini Spawn Locations

  • Dratini is a rare spawn in water below y level 32 and below light level 7
  • Dratini is an uncommon spawn near ocean ruins
  • It can also be found through fishing but I wouldn't recommend this method

    • Catching Dratini: Top Tips

    Dratini is traditionally a very difficult Pokemon to catch, but there is a trick that makes it much easier to obtain.

    The best way to find dratini is to search in caves below y level 32. The best strategy is to find a cave that has an underground pool or waterfall that dratini can spawn in. If you can't find a naturally generating underground pool you can create one with water buckets. When you do this, dratini is one of the few rare Pokemon that can spawn making it much easier to obtain.

    When using this strategy, I highly recommend using the PokeNav mod to ensure that there is enough water for dratini to spawn.

    Evolving Dratini to Dragonite

    Dratini evolves into Dragonair at level 30, and then into Dragonite at level 55.

    You can use experience shares to help raise your dratini's level more easily. Plains and snowy biomes are the best places to train if you want to level up your Pokemon quickly. I recommend targeting high-level pokemon like Tauros and Wyrdeer.
