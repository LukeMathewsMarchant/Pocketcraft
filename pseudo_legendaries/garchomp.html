Gible Spawn Locations

  • Gible is a rare spawn in badlands and mountain biomes
  • Gible is also an ultra-rare spawn anywhere in the overworld when the light level is below 7

    • Catching Gible: Top Tips

    Gible is actually one of the easier Pseudo-Legendary Pokemon to obtain in Cobblemon. It is a rare spawn in badlands and mountain biomes making it less exclusive than Pokemon like dratini and deino.

    The best strategy for catching Gible is to spend some time in either a mountain or badlands biome. Badlands biomes may be slightly less complicated as they are usually larger, but either biome will work. Since Gible is a rare, rather than ultra-rare spawn, it shouldn't take too long to come across one.

    The biggest danger to catching Gible is simply boredom. Patience will be the key to catching a Gible and as such I have found it's much easier to hunt for Gible when you are doing something else at the same time. I recommend training your Pokemon or building a structure in the either a badlands or mountain biome. These tasks will help you to not get bored as you wait for a Gible to spawn.

    My favorite structure to build in biomes while I'm hunting for rare Pokemon like Gible is a Pokemon Center. It helps the world come alive and feel more like a true Pokemon game. The video below gives a step-by-step tutorial on how to build a Pokemon Center if you would like to do so while hunting Gible.

    Evolving Gible to Garchomp

    Gible evolves into Gabite at level 24, and then into Garchomp at level 48.

    You can use experience shares to help raise your gible's level more easily. Plains and snowy biomes are the best places to train if you want to level up your Pokemon quickly. I recommend targeting high-level common pokemon like Tauros and Wyrdeer.
