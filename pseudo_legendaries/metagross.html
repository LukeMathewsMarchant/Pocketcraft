Beldum Spawn Locations

  • Beldum is a rare spawn in the end
  • Beldum is an ultra-rare spawn in dripstone caves and peak biomes

    • Catching Beldum: Top Tips

    Beldum is one of the easiest pseudo‑legendary Pokemon to catch in Cobblemon!

    I strongly recommend hunting for Beldum in the end. It is a rare spawn in the end but there are very few Pokemon that spawn in the end making it much easier to come by than rare spawns in other biomes

    The best strategy for finding a beldum is to just go raiding end cities in the end and you will probably find one well before you find elytra

    If you don't want to go to the end then dripstone cave and peak biomes can also work but it will take much longer to find one there. So you should come prepared to do something while you wait so you don't die of boredom. I recommend training your Pokemon or building something cool to pass the time while you wait for beldum to spawn.

    My favorite structure to build in biomes while I'm hunting for rare Pokemon like Beldum is a Pokemon Center. It helps the world come alive and feel more like a true Pokemon game. The video below gives a step-by-step tutorial on how to build a Pokemon Center if you would like to do so while hunting Beldum.

    Evolving Beldum to Metagross

    Beldum evolves into Metang at level 20, and then into Metagross at level 45.

    You can use experience shares to help raise your beldum's level more easily. Plains and snowy biomes are the best places to train if you want to level up your Pokemon quickly. I recommend targeting high-level common pokemon like Tauros and Wyrdeer.
